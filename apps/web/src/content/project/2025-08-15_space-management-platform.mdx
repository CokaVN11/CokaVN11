# Space Management Platform

## Overview

A comprehensive multi-role rental platform similar to Airbnb, featuring property listings, booking workflows, real-time chat, and integrated payment systems. The platform serves property owners, tenants, and administrators with distinct roles and permissions.

- **Booking Workflow**: Calendar integration with availability management
- **Real-time Chat**: In-app messaging between users and property owners
- **Payment Integration**: Secure wallet system for transactions
- **Review System**: Rating and feedback mechanism for completed bookings
